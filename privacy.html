Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/02/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fretio, a student-to-student marketplace exclusively for Chitkara campus. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data and our role as a platform connecting buyers and sellers within the university.

2. Our Role as a Platform

We do not sell products ourselves; all listings are posted by students. Our platform acts as a facilitator to connect students who want to rent, sell, or buy items. We do not take responsibility for product quality, transactions, or user interactions beyond providing a secure listing space.

3. No Return Policy

All transactions are final. We do not offer refunds or returns. If you have an issue with a product, you must contact the seller directly to resolve disputes. For any concerns or disputes, you can reach out to us at [Your Support Email] or through the Help & Support section of the website.
