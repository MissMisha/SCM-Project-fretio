About Us

Our Story

Fretio was born from a simple idea: to create a platform where students can easily buy, sell, or rent essential items from each other. As university students ourselves, we experienced the challenges of finding affordable supplies, especially during late-night study sessions or when immediate needs arose.

Founded in 2023, our mission is to build a community-focused marketplace that addresses the unique needs of student life. We understand that student budgets are tight, and sometimes you just need to borrow something for a short time rather than purchasing it outright.

Our platform connects students who have items to sell or rent with those who need them. From textbooks and calculators to medicines, food items, and clothing, Fretio provides a safe, convenient, and affordable way to access essentials right within your campus community.

What Makes Us Different

For Students, By Students: Fretio was created by students who understand the challenges of campus life, making our platform truly tailored to student needs.

Local Campus Communities: We focus on building micro-communities within each campus, ensuring quick access to items when you need them most.

Flexible Options: Whether you need to buy an item permanently or just rent it for a few days, Fretio gives you options that fit your needs and budget.

Responsible Consumption: By encouraging rental and second-hand purchases, we're helping reduce waste and promoting sustainable consumption among students.
